COMMISSION INFO

Payment
Prices are in USD. Negotiable due to the various add-ons.
I ask for payment upfront via PayPal.
Prices are currently being re-evaluated. Contact to negotiate.

Characters
There is a limit of two (2) characters per commission. If you want me to draw any more characters, it will count as a separate commission.
Currently open to drawing fandom characters and OCs (already existing characters).
Please provide references!

Progress Updates
For commissions that take longer to finish, I can use Sta.sh to privately upload progress (previews) of the artwork until completed.

Refunds
Can offer full or partial refund depending on progress (not started, draft, etc.).
When refunding, I am not responsible for any PayPal fees, if charged on your end.

Copyright
By commissioning me, you are agreeing to the following terms.
Guidelines

The rights of the artwork belong to me (as the independent artist).
The commissioner is allowed to use the artwork for personal use non-commercially, and to promote their characters (if OC) and repost the artwork (with artist credit).
The commissioner is not allowed to claim the artwork as their own, or sell the commissioned artwork without a written agreement.
