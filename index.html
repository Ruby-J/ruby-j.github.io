About Ruby J Shilan

Ruby is a hobbyist voice actor and artist. Having graduated with a degree in Computer Science, her favourite aspect of CS is multimedia. She is fascinated by the incorporation of story-telling and visuals within the same medium, whether it be a comic book or a video game. She hopes to create her own original series one day.

Ruby started YouTube in 2010 with uploads of Mario Paint Composer remixes of various video game and anime songs. Over time, the channel changed into a fandub one with the release of "The Gray Garden [+Voice Acting]" series in 2014.

Ruby is currently volunteering with the fan series "Danganronpa: Kill/Cure". With big dreams, she is figuring out what the next step forward should be.
