About Ruby J Shilan (RubyJCat)

Hello, and welcome to my page! I'm Ruby J, a hobbyist/indie voice actress. Having graduated with a degree in Computer Science, my favourite aspect of CS is multimedia. I'm fascinated by the incorporation of story-telling and visuals within the same medium, whether it be a comic book or a video game. I hope to create my own original series one day.

I started a YouTube channel in 2010 with uploads of Mario Paint Composer remixes of various video game and anime songs. The channel's focus changed to voice acting content starting in 2014 when I did a fandub of "The Gray Garden".

I landed my first indie game voice over role in 2017 for "The Tenth Line". I'm currently volunteering as a Voice Actor Manager for the fan web series "Danganronpa: Kill/Cure".
