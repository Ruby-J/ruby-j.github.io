About Ruby J Shilan (RubyJCat)

Hello, and welcome to my page! I'm Ruby J, a hobbyist voice actress and artist. Having graduated with a degree in Computer Science, my favourite aspect of CS is multimedia. I'm fascinated by the incorporation of story-telling and visuals within the same medium, whether it be a comic book or a video game. I ultimately hope to create my own original series one day.

I started a YouTube channel in 2010 with uploads of Mario Paint Composer remixes of various video game and anime songs. Over time, the channel developed into a more voice acting-focused channel with the release of "The Gray Garden [+Voice Acting]" series in 2014.

I am currently involved in a couple of projects such as "Danganronpa: Kill/Cure".
